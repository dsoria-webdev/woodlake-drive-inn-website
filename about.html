About Our Restaurant!

Woodlake Drive Inn is a classic fast food and to-go restaurant located in the heart of the town of Woodlake. We offer a wide variety of options including cheeseburgers, pastrami sandwiches, crispy fried pickles, ice cream, and many other mouth-watering dishes to choose from. Our valued customers rave about our hearty portions and flavor-packed food, and we are certain that you too will fall in love with our dishes! Whether you are in town for a quick snack or a hearty fast food combo, we will be sure to please!
