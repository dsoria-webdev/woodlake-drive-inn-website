About Our Restraunt!

Woodlake Drive Inn is a classic fast food and to go restaurant located in the heart of the town of Woodlake. We offer a wide variety of various options including cheeseburgers, pastrami sandwiches, crispy fried pickles, ice cream, and so many other mouth watering dishes to choose from. Our valued customers rave about our hearty portions and the flavor packed food and we are certain that you too will fall in love with our dishes! Whether you are in town for a quick snack or a heart fast food combo, we will be sure to please!.
